View and Filter all Price Attributes
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

To view all price attributes, navigate to **Products > Price Attributes** in the main menu.

Preview:

.. image:: /user_guide/img/products/price_attributes/PriceAttributes.png
   :class: with-border

You can perform the following actions with every item in the Price Attributes list:

 * `Create a Price Attribute <create>`_: Click **Create Price Attribute** to the top right from the list.

 * Bulk delete price attributes: In a bulk select to the top left, select one of the following options: All, All Visible, None. On the top right of the list (grid), click bulk |IcMore| *more actions* menu and click the |IcDelete| to remove all selected price attributes.

 * `View price attribute details <view>`_: Click on the item to open its details page.

   OR

   Alternatively: Hover over the |IcMore| *more actions* menu to the right of the item and click |IcView| to open its details page.

 * Delete a price attribute: Hover over the |IcMore| *more actions* menu to the right of the item and click |IcDelete| to remove the price attribute.

 * `Edit a price attribute <edit>`_: Hover over the |IcMore| *more actions* menu to the right of the item and click the |IcEdit| to start editing its details.

 * Filter landing pages by Price Attribute name or Field Name.
