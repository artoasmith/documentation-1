Export Products
===============

.. note::

   All existing product records are exported at once.

1. In the main menu, navigate to **Products > Products**. The product list opens.
2. Click **Export** on the top right.

As soon as the export job finishes, you will receive an email with the link to download the .csv file.
The exported file will contain all product record fields marked for export in the Product entity settings.
