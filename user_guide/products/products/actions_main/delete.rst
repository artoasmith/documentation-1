Delete a Product
----------------

.. contents::
   :local:

.. _doc--products--actions--delete--fromgrid:

Delete a Product (From the Product List)
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

1. In the main menu, navigate to **Products > Products**. The product list opens.

.. TODO add image

2. Choose the product that you need to delete, click the |IcMore| **More Options** menu at the end of the row, and then click the |IcDelete| **Delete** icon.
#. In the confirmation dialog, click **Yes, Delete**.

.. _doc--products--actions--delete--multiple:

Delete Multiple Products (From the Product List)
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

1. In the main menu, navigate to **Products > Products**. The product list opens.
2. Select the check boxes in front of the products that you need to delete, click the |IcMore| **More Options** menu at the end of list header, and then click |IcDelete| **Delete**.

   .. note:: To select bulk of items quickly, use *All* (to select all products) or *All Visible* (to select only products that are visible on the page now) from the list above the check boxes.

      .. image:: /user_guide/img/products/products/bulk_select.png

      Then, select Delete in the |IcMore| **More Options** menu at the end of the header.

      .. image:: /user_guide/img/products/products/bulk_delete_2.png

      To clear the selection, use the *None* option from the list above the check boxes.

#. In the confirmation dialog, click **Yes, Delete**.


.. _doc--products--actions--delete--fromviewpage:

Delete a Product (From the Product Details)
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

1. In the main menu, navigate to **Products > Products**. The product list opens.
2. Click the product that you need to delete. The product view page opens.

.. TODO add image

3. Click **Delete** on the top right.
#. In the confirmation dialog, click **Yes, Delete**.
