Duplicate a Product
-------------------



.. _doc--products--actions--duplicate--fromgrid:

From the Product List
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

1. In the main menu, navigate to **Products > Products**. The product list opens.
2. Choose the product that you need to duplicate, click the |IcMore| **More Options** menu at the end of the row, and then click the |IcClone| **Duplicate** icon.

The duplicated product opens. It has the **Disabled** status and its SKU is automatically generated by adding a hyphen and a duplicate version number to the original SKU.
Now you can edit the duplicated product to customize its settings as required.


.. _doc--products--actions--duplicate--fromviewpage:

From the Product View Page
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

1. In the main menu, navigate to **Products > Products**. The product list opens.
2. Click the product that you want to duplicate.

   Alternatively, you can click the |IcMore| **More Options** menu at the end of the row, and then click the |IcView| **View** icon.

   The product view page opens.

3. Click **Duplicate** on the top right.
