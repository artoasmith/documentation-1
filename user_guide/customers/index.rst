Customers
=========

In OroCommerce, **customers** represent companies who buy products using the OroCommerce storefront. **Customer users** are actual people who act on behalf of companies (i.e. customers) and have a limited set of permissions which depend on their role and function in the customer organization. You can manage customer users and roles in the OroCommerce management console, or you can delegate this function to the customer who will access user and role management in the OroCommerce storefront.
