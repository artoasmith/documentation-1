Edit a Quote
------------

.. begin

To edit the Quote:

#. Navigate to **Sales > Quotes** in the main menu.

#. Hover over the |IcMore| **More Options** menu to the right of the item and click |IcEdit| to start editing its details.

#. Update the *Quote General Information*, *Line Items*, *Shipping Address*, or *Shipping Information*. See `Create a Quote <create>`_ section for detailed information on the available options.

#. Click **Save** on the top right of the page.

The quote is updated.

.. finish
