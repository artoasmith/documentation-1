View and Filter all Quotes
--------------------------

To view all Quotes, navigate to **Sales > Quotes** in the main menu.

.. include:: /user_guide/overview/sales/quotes_overview.rst
  :start-after: begin

Preview:

.. image:: /user_guide/img/sales/quotes/Quotes.png
   :class: with-border

.. note:: To handle big volume of data, use page switcher, increase *View Per Page* or use `filters <user-guide-ui-components-grid-filters>`_ to narrow down the list to the codes you need.

The following information about the Quotes is available:

* Quote #
* Owner
* Customer User
* Customer
* Internal Status
* Locked
* Expired
* Valid Until
* PO Number
* DNSLT
* Create At
* Update At

**Next steps**

You can perform the following actions with every item of the Quotes list:

 * `View a Quote details <view>`_: Click on the item to open its details page.

   OR

   Alternatively: Hover over the |IcMore| **More Options** menu to the right of the item and click |IcView| to open its details page.

 * `Edit a Quote details <edit>`_: Hover over the |IcMore| **More Options** menu to the right of the item and click |IcEdit| to start editing its details.

 * `Expire a Quote <edit>`_: Click |IcExpireQuote| to the right of the item to start editing its details.

 * Delete a Quote: Hover over the |IcMore| **More Options** menu to the right of the item and click |IcDelete| to remove the Quote.

You can perform the following actions on the Quotes page:

 * `Create Quote <./create>`_
