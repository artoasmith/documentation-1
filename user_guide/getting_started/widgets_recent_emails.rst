Recent Emails Dashboard Widget
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

Another way of reaching your emails is through a **Recent Emails
widget** on your dashboard.

The Recent Emails widget added to your dashboard will have the following
features:

-  **Inbox tab**

-  **Sent tab**

-  **Unread emails tab**

-  **Reply**

-  **View**
