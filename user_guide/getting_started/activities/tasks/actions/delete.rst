Delete a Task
-------------

.. contents:: :local:

From the Tasks Grid / My Tasks Page
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

.. begin_single

To delete a task from a list of tasks:

1. Open the list of tasks:

   - To remove a task of any assignee, open a general list of tasks. For this, in the main menu, navigate to **Activities>Tasks**. A page with the general list of tasks opens.
   - To remove a task assigned to you, open a list of your tasks. For this, in the user menu, click **My Tasks**.

2. On the task list, click the |IcEllipsisH| **More Options** menu at the end of the row of the task that you would like to delete and then click the |IcDelete| **Delete** icon.

.. image:: /user_guide/img/activities/tasks/tasks_delete1.png

3. In the **Deletion Confirmation** dialog, click **Yes, Delete**.


.. finish_single

From the Task View Page
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Alternatively, you can delete a task from the task view page by clicking the **Delete** button in the upper-right corner of the page.

.. image:: /user_guide/img/activities/tasks/tasks_delete2.png

From the Related Entity View Page
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Entity records that are specified in a task context have the task displayed in the **Activity** sections of their view pages.

To delete a task when viewing related record details:

1. On the entity record view page, click **Activity**.

2. In the **Activity** section, click the |IcEllipsisH| **More Options** menu at the end of the row with the task you would like to delete and then click |IcDelete| **Delete Task**.

   .. image:: /user_guide/img/activities/tasks/tasks_delete3.png

3. In the **Deletion Confirmation** dialog, click **Yes, Delete**.



From the Assignee's Profile
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

When user is assigned a task, it appears in the **Tasks** subsection of the **Additional Information** section in the user profile.

To delete a task from the assignee's profile page:

1. Open the task assignee's profile.

2. Click **Additional Information** and navigate to the **Tasks** subsection.

3. In the task list, click the |IcEllipsisH| **More Options** menu at the end of the row with the task you would like to delete and then click the |IcDelete| **Delete** icon.

   .. image:: /user_guide/img/activities/tasks/activities_tasks_actions_delete_userpage.png

4. In the **Deletion Confirmation** dialog, click **Yes, Delete**.


From the My Calendar page / Today's Calendar Widget
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

To delete a task from a calendar (in the **Today's calendar** widget or on your calendar page):

1. Click the task in the calendar.
2. On the **Task Information** card, click **Delete**.

   .. image:: /user_guide/img/activities/tasks/activities_tasks_actions_delete_calendar.png

3. In the **Delete Confirmation** dialog, click **Yes, Delete**.

.. _doc-activities-tasks-actions-massdelete:

Delete Multiple Tasks
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

.. begin_bulk

To delete a bulk of tasks from the page with general list of tasks or from the list on the **My Tasks** page:

1. Open the list of tasks:

   - To remove tasks of any assignee, open a general list of tasks. For this, in the main menu, navigate to **Activities>Tasks**. A page with the general list of tasks opens.
   - To remove tasks assigned to you, open a list of your tasks. For this, in the user menu, click **My Tasks**.

2. On the task list, select check boxes in front of the tasks that you would like to delete.

3. Click the |IcEllipsisH| **More Options** menu at the end of the grid header row and then click |IcDelete| **Delete**.

   .. image:: /user_guide/img/activities/tasks/tasks_massdelete.png

4. In the **Delete Confirmation** dialog, click **Yes, Delete**.
