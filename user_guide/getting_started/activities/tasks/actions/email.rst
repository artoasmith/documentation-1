Send an Email Related to a Task
-------------------------------

.. begin_email

1. In the main menu, navigate to **Activities>Tasks**.

2. On the task list, click the required task.

   Alternatively, you can click the |IcEllipsisH| **More Options** menu at the end of the corresponding row and then click the |IcView| **View** icon.

3. On the task view page, click the **Send Email** button in the upper-right corner of the page.

4. In the **Send Email** dialog, specify the required data. For help, see :ref:`Compose A New Email <doc-activities-emails-actions-compose>`.

  .. image:: /user_guide/img/activities/tasks/tasks_sendemail.png

5. Click **Send**.
