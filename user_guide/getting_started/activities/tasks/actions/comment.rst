Comment on a Task
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

.. contents:: :local:

.. _doc-activities-tasks-actions-comment-viewpage:

From the Task View Page
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

.. begin

To comment on a task when viewing the task details:

1. In the main menu, navigate to **Activities>Tasks**.

2. On the task list, click the required task.

   Alternatively, you can click the |IcEllipsisH| **More Options** menu at the end of the corresponding row and then click the |IcView| **View** icon.

3. On the task view page, click **Comments**.

4. In the **Comments** section, click the **Add Comment** button in the lower-right corner.

   .. image:: /user_guide/img/activities/tasks/tasks_comment.png

#. In the **Add Comment** dialog, enter your comment. If rich text functionality is enabled for you, you can format your comment in the editor. You can also add an attachment to your comment.

   .. image:: /user_guide/img/activities/tasks/tasks_comment_dialog.png

#. Click the **Add** button.

   Your comment appears in the **Comments** section of the task view page.

.. tip::
   You can edit or remove your comment: find your comment and click the |IcEllipsisH| **More Options** menu at the end of the corresponding row. Then click the |IcEdit| **Edit** / |IcDelete| **Delete** icon.

.. finish

From the Related Entity View Page
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Entity records that are specified in the task context have this task displayed in the **Activity** sections of their view pages.

You can add a comment to a task directly from the view page of a related entity.

1. On the entity record view page, click **Activity**.
2. In the **Activity** section, choose a task to comment, and click it to see details.
#. Click the **Add Comment** button in the lower-right corner.

   .. image:: /user_guide/img/activities/tasks/tasks_comment_fromactivity.png

#. In the **Add Comment** dialog, enter your comment. If rich text functionality is enabled for you, you can format your comment in the editor. You can also add an attachment to your comment.

   .. image:: /user_guide/img/activities/tasks/tasks_comment_dialog.png

#. Click the **Add** button.

   Your comment appears in the **Comments** section of the task details.

   .. image:: /user_guide/img/activities/tasks/tasks_comment_fromactivity2.png
