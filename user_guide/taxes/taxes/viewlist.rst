View and Filter Tax Rates
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

.. begin

To view all tax rates, navigate to **Taxes > Tax Rates** in the main menu.

.. image:: /user_guide/img/taxes/taxes/tax_rates_all.png
   :alt: The page of all available tax rates

.. note:: To handle big a volume of data, use the page switcher, increase *View Per Page* or use filters to narrow down the list to just the codes you need.

The following information about the tax rates is available in the tax rate list:

+-------------+------------------------------------------+
| Name        | Description                              |
+=============+==========================================+
| CODE        | The unique identifier of the tax rate.   |
+-------------+------------------------------------------+
| DESCRIPTION | Detailed description of the tax rate.    |
+-------------+------------------------------------------+
| CREATED AT  | Date when the tax rate was created.      |
+-------------+------------------------------------------+
| UPDATED AT  | Date when the tax rate was updated last. |
+-------------+------------------------------------------+

You can perform the following actions with every item in the tax rates list:

 * `View Tax Rate details <view>`_ --- Click on the item to open its details page.

   Alternatively: Hover over the |IcMore| **More Options** menu to the right of the item and click |IcView| to open its details page.

 * `Edit Tax Rate details <edit>`_ --- Hover over the |IcMore| **More Options** menu to the right of the item and click |IcEdit| to start editing its details.

 * Delete a Tax Rate: Hover over the |IcMore| **More Options** menu to the right of the item and click |IcDelete| to remove the Tax Rate.

.. stop
