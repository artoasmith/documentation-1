Address Book
============

The following information covers the Address Book section of the Account menu in OroCommerce front store.

.. _frontstore-guide--address-overview:

.. begin

.. contents:: :local:
   :depth: 2

To locate Address Book:

1. Click **Account** in the :ref:`user menu <frontstore-guide--navigation-user-menu>` on the top left of the page.
2. Under **Account**, Click **Address Book**.

Address Book has two sections:

1. All Company Addresses
2. All My Addresses
