Calendar Events
===============

In Oro application (e.g. in OroCRM and in OroCommerce management console), you can create and monitor planned activities as calendar events. These planned activities may be linked to the related data in Oro application (e.g. a demo may be linked to the opportunity in OroCRM and to the request for a quote in OroCommerce).
